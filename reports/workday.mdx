title: "Workday Reports"
description: "Shift and workday-level analytics for reconciliation."
---

**Workday Reports** show sales and payments broken down by shift or business day — useful for end-of-day cash reconciliation and shift handovers.

## What's in a Workday Report

Each workday entry shows:

- **Open / Close time** — when the shift started and ended.
- **Opened by / Closed by** — which staff member managed the shift.
- **Total Sales** — gross revenue for the shift.
- **Payment Breakdown** — how much was collected per payment type.
- **Void / Refund Total** — cancellations and refunds within the shift.
- **Expected Cash** — calculated cash in drawer (opening float + cash sales − refunds).

## View a Workday

Click any row to expand the full breakdown for that shift.

## Print the Report

Click **Print** on an open workday row to print the shift summary for the physical cash drawer record.

## Reconciliation Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Count the cash drawer">
    At end of shift, count the actual cash in the drawer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare to Expected Cash">
    Open the workday report and check the **Expected Cash** figure. Any difference is the over/short for the shift.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close the workday">
    Click **Close Workday** and enter the actual cash counted. The difference is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>
