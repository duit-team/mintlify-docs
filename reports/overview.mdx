The **Reports** section gives you a summary of your business performance — revenue, order counts, tax collected, and payment breakdowns.

## Available Reports

| Report | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **Sales Summary** | Total revenue, order count, average order value by day/week/month |
| **Payment Breakdown** | Revenue split by payment type (Cash, Card, Online) |
| **Tax Report** | Tax collected per rate |
| **Product Sales** | Best and worst selling products |
| **Category Sales** | Revenue per menu category |

## Date Range

Use the **Date** picker at the top to filter any report by a custom date range, or use the quick shortcuts: Today, This Week, This Month.

## Export

Click **Export CSV** on any report to download the data for use in Excel or another tool.

<Tip>
  For shift-by-shift breakdowns, see [Workday Reports](/reports/workday).
</Tip>
