---
title: "Employees"
description: "Manage staff records separate from system login accounts."
---

**Employees** are staff records used for scheduling, time tracking, and sales attribution. They are separate from **Users** (system login accounts) — an employee may or may not have a login.

## Create an Employee

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Manage → Employees">
    Click **Employees** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Employee">
    Fill in the employee's name, contact details, and any other required fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Link to a User Account

If this employee also has a system login, open their record and link it to the corresponding **User** account. This enables sales and shift reports to be attributed to them by name.

## Deactivate

Toggle **Active** off to mark an employee as inactive (e.g. when they leave). Their historical records are preserved.
