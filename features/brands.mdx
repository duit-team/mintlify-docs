---
title: "Brands"
description: "Manage product brands used in your menu."
---

**Brands** let you group products under a named brand (e.g. *Coca-Cola*, *House Label*). They appear as filters in the menu builder and on reports.

## Create a Brand

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Manage → Brands">
    Click **Brands** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Brand">
    Enter a **Name** and toggle **Active** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assign to Products

Go to **Menu Builder → Products**, open a product, and select a brand from the **Brand** dropdown.
