---

**Settings** contains global configuration that applies across your entire account.

## General Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Business Name** | Appears on receipts, e-invoices, and the customer-facing online menu |
| **Logo** | Displayed on receipts and the online ordering page |
| **Currency** | The currency used for all prices and reports |
| **Timezone** | Controls report date grouping and scheduled jobs |
| **Receipt Footer** | Custom text printed at the bottom of every receipt |

## Notification Settings

Configure email or in-app alerts for key events — low stock, daily summary, failed payments, etc.

## Saving Changes

All settings are saved per-field. Changes take effect immediately — no restart required.

<Warning>
  Changing the **Currency** does not convert existing prices. Update your product prices manually after a currency change.
</Warning>
