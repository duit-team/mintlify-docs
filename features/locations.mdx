---
title: "Locations"
description: "Manage physical branches or outlets under your account."
---

A **Location** represents a physical outlet or branch. Each location has its own menu, settings, and reports.

## Create a Location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Manage → Locations">
    Click **Locations** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Location">
    Fill in:
    - **Name** — the branch name, e.g. `KL Central` or `Outlet 2`.
    - **Address** — displayed on receipts and e-invoices.
    - **Phone / Email** — contact details for this branch.
    - **Active** — inactive locations are hidden from reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit a Location

Click the **Edit** icon. All fields including address and contact details can be updated. Changes take effect immediately on new receipts.

<Warning>
  Location details (name, address) appear on printed receipts and e-invoices. Update them before the next business day if they change.
</Warning>
