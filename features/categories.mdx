---
title: "Categories"
description: "Organise your products into categories for the POS and online menu."
---

**Categories** group products on the POS screen and the online ordering menu. Customers browse by category; kitchen routing rules can also filter by category.

## Create a Category

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Menu Builder → Categories">
    Categories can also be managed from the **Menu Builder** page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Category">
    Fill in:
    - **Name** — displayed on the POS and online menu.
    - **Colour** — optional, used to highlight the category button on the POS.
    - **Active** — toggle off to hide from the POS without deleting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reorder Categories

Drag rows by the sort handle to change the order on the POS. The first active category is selected by default.

## Add Products to a Category

Go to **Menu Builder → Products**, open a product, and select a category in the **Category** field.
