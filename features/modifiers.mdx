---
title: "Modifiers"
description: "Set up product options like size, extras, or cooking preferences."
---

**Modifiers** let customers customise a product — e.g. choose a size, add toppings, or specify cooking level. They are grouped into **Modifier Groups** and attached to products.

## Create a Modifier Group

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Manage → Modifiers">
    Click **Modifiers** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Group">
    Enter a name, e.g. `Size` or `Add-ons`. Set:
    - **Min selections** — minimum choices required (e.g. `1` for mandatory size).
    - **Max selections** — maximum allowed (e.g. `1` for single-select, `5` for multi-select).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Options">
    Click **Add Option** inside the group. Enter the option name and any price adjustment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create Group**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Attach to a Product

Go to **Menu Builder → Products**, open a product, and in the **Modifiers** section select the groups that apply to this product.

## Common Examples

| Group | Min | Max | Options |
|-------|-----|-----|---------|
| Size | 1 | 1 | Small (+$0), Medium (+$1), Large (+$2) |
| Add-ons | 0 | 5 | Extra cheese (+$0.50), Bacon (+$1.50) |
| Cooking Level | 0 | 1 | Rare, Medium, Well Done |
