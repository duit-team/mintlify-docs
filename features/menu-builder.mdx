The **Menu Builder** is the central place to build and maintain your menu. It combines categories, products, and modifier groups in a single view.

## Layout

The Menu Builder has two main panels:

- **Left — Categories**: Your category list. Click a category to view its products on the right.
- **Right — Products**: Products in the selected category. Expand a product to see its modifier groups.

## Adding Items

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="New Category" icon="folder-plus" href="/features/categories">
    Click **New Category** at the top of the category panel.
  </Card>
  <Card title="New Product" icon="plus" href="/features/products">
    Select a category first, then click **New Product** in the product panel.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Modifier Groups" icon="list" href="/features/modifiers">
    Expand a product and click **Add Modifier Group** to attach options.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bulk Pricing" icon="tag" href="/features/bulk-pricing">
    Use Bulk Pricing to override prices for a specific context.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Reordering

Drag any category or product row by its handle to change the display order on the POS.

## Context View

Use the **Context** dropdown at the top to preview how the menu looks with overrides applied (e.g. delivery pricing, happy-hour names).
