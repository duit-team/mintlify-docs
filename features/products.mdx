---
title: "Products"
description: "Add and manage the items sold at your POS and online."
---

**Products** are the items customers can order. Each product belongs to a category and can have modifiers, a tax rate, and context-specific price overrides.

## Create a Product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Menu Builder → Products">
    Select a category in the Menu Builder first to scope where the product appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Product">
    Fill in:
    - **Name** — displayed on the POS and receipts.
    - **Code / SKU** — optional internal reference.
    - **Price** — the base selling price (excluding tax unless the price is tax-inclusive).
    - **Category** — which category this product belongs to.
    - **Tax** — the tax rate to apply. See [Tax](/features/tax).
    - **Brand** — optional. See [Brands](/features/brands).
    - **Active** — toggle off to hide from the POS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add modifiers (optional)">
    In the **Modifiers** section, attach modifier groups (e.g. Size, Add-ons). See [Modifiers](/features/modifiers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create** (or **Save** on the edit page).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit a Product

Click the **Edit** icon on any product row. All fields are editable. Price changes take effect on the next order.

## Bulk Price Overrides

To set a different price for a specific context (e.g. delivery), use [Bulk Pricing](/features/bulk-pricing) instead of editing the base price.

## Delete a Product

Click **Delete** on the product row. Products referenced by existing tickets cannot be deleted — deactivate them instead.
