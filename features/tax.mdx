---
title: "Tax"
description: "Configure tax rates and apply them to products."
---

**Tax rates** define the percentage charged on sales. You can create multiple rates (e.g. SST 6%, Service Charge 10%) and assign them per product or globally.

## Create a Tax Rate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Manage → Tax">
    Click **Tax** in the left sidebar under Manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Tax">
    Fill in:
    - **Code** — short identifier, e.g. `SST` or `SVC`.
    - **Name** — display name, e.g. `Sales & Service Tax` or `Service Charge`.
    - **Rate (%)** — the percentage as a number, e.g. `6` for 6%.
    - **Active** — inactive taxes are excluded from all calculations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit or Delete

Click the **Edit** icon to change any field. Click **Delete** to remove a tax rate.

<Warning>
  Deleting a tax rate that has been applied to past transactions will **not** remove it from historical records, but it will stop appearing on new transactions.
</Warning>

## Applying Tax to Products

After creating a tax rate, go to **Menu Builder → Products** and assign the desired tax rate to each product. Tax is calculated per line item on the ticket.
