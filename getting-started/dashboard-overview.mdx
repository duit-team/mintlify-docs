## Top Navigation

- **Search** — Find any record quickly across the system.
- **Notifications** — Alerts for important events (e.g. low stock, failed payments).
- **Account menu** — Change your password or sign out.

## Sidebar Sections

### Manage
Configuration for your business: tax rates, payment types, order modes, printers, and more. Changes here take effect immediately.

### Users & Roles
Create staff accounts, assign roles, and control permissions per role.

### Reports
Daily, weekly, and monthly summaries of sales, payments, and voids.

### Online Ordering
Generate and manage QR codes for table ordering. Requires Stripe to be configured under Payment Types.

## Help Links

Every page has a **?** icon in the top-right of the page header. Clicking it opens the relevant help article for that page directly.
