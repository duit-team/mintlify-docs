## Sign In

1. Open your dashboard URL in a browser.
2. Enter your email and password provided by your administrator.
3. Click **Sign In**.

<Tip>
  If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password?** on the login page to receive a reset link by email.
</Tip>

## What You See After Login

After signing in you land on the **Dashboard**. The left sidebar contains all main sections:

| Section | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Manage** | Configure tax, payment types, printers, and other settings |
| **Users** | Manage staff accounts and roles |
| **Reports** | View sales and transaction summaries |
| **Online Ordering** | Manage QR codes and online menu |

## Switching Tenants

If your account has access to more than one location, a location selector appears at the top of the sidebar. Click it to switch.
