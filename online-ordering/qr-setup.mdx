---
title: "QR Code Setup"
description: "Generate table QR codes for contactless ordering."
---

QR codes let customers scan and order from their phone. Each QR code maps to a specific table and venue.

## Prerequisites

Before generating QR codes, make sure:
- You have at least one **Table** set up under Manage → Tables.
- Stripe is configured in a Payment Type (see [Stripe Setup](/online-ordering/stripe-setup)).
- Your online menu is published.

## Generate a QR Code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Online Ordering → QR Codes">
    Click **QR Codes** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Table">
    Choose the table you want to generate a code for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or Print">
    Click **Download QR** to get a PNG, or **Print** to send directly to a printer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Test the Flow

1. Scan your QR code with a phone camera.
2. Browse the menu and add items to the cart.
3. Proceed to checkout — a Stripe payment page opens.
4. Complete the test payment using a [Stripe test card](https://stripe.com/docs/testing#cards).
5. Confirm the order appears in your dashboard.

<Tip>
  Use `4242 4242 4242 4242` with any future expiry and any CVC as a Stripe test card number.
</Tip>

## Customise the Landing Page

Go to **Online Ordering → Settings** to upload your logo, set a welcome message, and configure operating hours.
