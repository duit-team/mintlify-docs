---
title: "Stripe Setup"
description: "Connect Stripe to accept online payments through QR ordering."
---

Stripe processes all online card payments. You need a Stripe account and your API keys.

## Get Your Stripe Keys

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Stripe">
    Go to [dashboard.stripe.com](https://dashboard.stripe.com) and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Developers → API Keys">
    In the top-right, click **Developers**, then **API keys**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your Secret Key">
    Click **Reveal live secret key** and copy it. Keep this private — never share it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Stripe in the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Manage → Payment Types">
    Open the Payment Types page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit or create a payment type for Stripe">
    Click **Edit** on an existing card payment type, or create a new one (e.g. Code: `STRIPE`, Name: `Online Payment`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Stripe processor">
    In the **Processors** section, select **Stripe** from the dropdown and click **Add**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    - **Secret Key** — paste your Stripe secret key.
    - **Webhook Secret** — see below for how to get this.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save Processors**. Your keys are encrypted before storage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up the Stripe Webhook

The webhook ensures orders are confirmed even if a customer closes their browser before the redirect completes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Stripe → Developers → Webhooks">
    Click **Add endpoint**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your endpoint URL">
    ```
    https://yourdomain.com/api/YOUR_TENANT/qr/payment/webhook
    ```
    Replace `YOUR_TENANT` with your tenant domain name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select events">
    Add these two events:
    - `checkout.session.completed`
    - `checkout.session.expired`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the Webhook Secret">
    After saving, click **Reveal** next to the signing secret (starts with `whsec_`). Paste it into the **Webhook Secret** field in the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Use **live mode** keys for production. Use **test mode** keys while testing — they look the same but are labelled `sk_test_` vs `sk_live_`.
</Warning>

## Test the Integration

Use Stripe's test card `4242 4242 4242 4242` with any future expiry date. A successful payment should appear in your dashboard and create a ticket automatically.
