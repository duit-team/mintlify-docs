---
title: "Login Problems"
description: "Can't sign in? Here's what to check."
---

## Forgot Password

Click **Forgot password?** on the login page. Enter your email address and check your inbox for a reset link. Check your spam folder if it does not arrive within a minute.

## Account Is Inactive

If you see *"Your account is inactive"*, contact your administrator. They can reactivate your account under **Users → Users → Edit → Active**.

## Wrong Tenant

The login page URL includes your tenant domain (e.g. `app.yourdomain.com/my-business/login`). Make sure you are on the correct URL for your organisation.

## Locked Out After Too Many Attempts

Wait 15 minutes and try again. If the problem persists, ask your administrator to reset your password manually from the Users page.

## Still Can't Log In?

Contact your system administrator with:
- Your email address.
- The exact error message shown.
- The URL you are trying to access.
