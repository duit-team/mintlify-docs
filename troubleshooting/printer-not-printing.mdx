title: "Printer Not Printing"
description: "Diagnose and fix printer connectivity problems."
---

## Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check power and network">
    Make sure the printer is on and connected to the same local network as your POS device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the IP address">
    Print a self-test page from the printer (usually hold the feed button at startup). Confirm the IP matches what is saved in **Manage → Printers**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a test print">
    In **Manage → Printers**, click **Test Print** next to the printer. Watch for any error message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check port 9100">
    Most receipt printers use port `9100`. Confirm this matches the **Port** field in the printer settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check routing rules">
    Go to the **Routing Rules** tab. Confirm a rule exists that targets this printer for the relevant category or product.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Printer Prints But Wrong Items

The routing rules may be too broad or conflicting. Check:
- The rule **condition** matches only the intended categories.
- No higher-priority rule is redirecting the job to another printer.
- The **Priority** field — lower numbers run first.
